A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a tool used to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height. It provides an estimate of body fat and helps classify individuals into different weight categories such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. However, BMI does not directly measure body fat percentage or distribution.

Limitations of BMI:


  • Muscle Mass: Athletes or people with higher muscle mass may have a higher BMI, which may not indicate excess body fat.


  • Body Composition: It doesn't account for body fat percentage, distribution, or other factors like bone density.


  • Age and Gender: BMI doesn't differentiate between men and women, and it may not be accurate for children or the elderly.
